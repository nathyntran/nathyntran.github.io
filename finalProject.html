Final Project Page

My partner and I created a side scroller game that allows the player to control the character's movements through the use of the up and down arrow keys. The character has different animations for each key press with different hit boxes. The goal of the game is to kill as many dragons as you can and survive past a certain time limit. If the character hits a dragon, however, they lose health. To kill the dragons, the character must obtain the sword powerup to not take damage and kill the dragons. If the player presses the down arrow, the character goes into a crouch, and can not get hit, but the player can not pickup or kill any dragons in that state. The dragons will pick up speed every so often and slow back down, so be mindful of the speed changes!
